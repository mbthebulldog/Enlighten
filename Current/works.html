Eckhart Tolle

Author ofThe Power of Now, A New Earth, and several other treatises on enlightenment, Tolle was a scholar at Cambridge when a radical inner transformation altered his path. He now travels the world spreading his spiritual lightness with those who wish to find peace and joy.

Don Miguel Ruiz

Ruiz was raised in rural Mexico by practitioners of ancient Toltec traditions, which he combines with scientific perspectives to form a new philosophy of personal authenticity. In The Four Agreements, he lays bare the structure of this philosophy, detailing the spiritual state of the world and precise methods for the attainment of enlightenment.

Osho

Osho was a mystic and spiritual leader who traveled throughout India as a professor of philosophy. He advocated for a broader understanding and acceptance of human sexuality, and has been given the monicker "sex guru". His essays and treatises on egoless sex, such as From Sex to Super-Consciousness, continue to be published worldwide by over 200 publishers in more than 60 languages.
