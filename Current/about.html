My name is Maxwell Valentine, and I'm a lot of things. Many of those things are nouns, like professions, roles, and even physical categories. For instance, I could tell you I'm a man, a graduate student, and a lover of weird music, but none of that really matters. The only thing I really am is alive. But guess what? That means we've got a lot in common!

If you're here looking for enlightenment, I hope you find it. I'll help you every step of the way. And if you're here to figure out how to love and be loved, then my words are the same. You got this!

Don't hesitate to ask questions, posit ideas, or even criticize. This is a place of respect and communication, so don't hold back. I want to hear you more than I want you to hear me.

I try to post every month, sometimes twice. That being said, try not to form too many expectations. Just let life lead you and have faith. I'll always be here when you need me. ;)
